摘 要
随着产业数智转型的不断深入， 云数据中心、 边缘节点与终端设备构成的三层异构
计算拓扑逐步形成， 云边端协同跨域计算将分散于不同网络域的算力与存储纳入统一调
度， 为云边端跨域分布式训练开辟了新的资源整合路径。 课题组据此研发了云边端跨域
虚拟存储空间系统，打通了三层节点间的基本数据访问通路。
然而， 云边端环境中的广域链路普遍存在带宽受限与传播延迟显著的问题， 使得训
练全流程的 IO效率面临严峻考验。 具体而言来说， 元数据请求需频繁穿越跨域链路， 目
录遍历和文件属性查询的开销成倍放大， 严重拖慢训练冷启动； 训练样本从远端加载到
本地的速度跟不上 GPU消耗节奏， 计算单元长时间等待数据就绪而空转； 检查点大体积
状态文件的同步写入会阻塞训练迭代数十秒乃至数分钟， 且边缘节点异构的带宽与存储
条件也使故障后的快速恢复难以保障。
因此，本文面向云边端跨域分布式训练中的 IO性能瓶颈，围绕元数据预取、数据
预热和检查点写入与恢复三条路径展开存储层面的加速研究， 旨在优化跨域数据访问通
路、提升训练效率。本文的具体研究工作和主要成果如下：
（1） 针对元数据跨域访问开销过大的问题， 设计并实现面向训练冷启动的元数据批
量预取与多级缓存机制。首先，在数据集初始化阶段，通过结合异步递归目录树与请求
聚合， 在单轮内完成全量元数据的拉取； 其次， 通过内存—磁盘—共享内存三级缓存， 使
预取到的元数据在后续直接复用，消除重复的网络请求。实验结果表明，该机制在不同
文件规模和延迟条件下均显著降低了元数据访问开销， 冷启动速度和后续轮次的缓存命
中效率均得到明显提升。
（2）针对跨域样本加载拖慢训练节奏、导致 GPU算力空转的问题，设计并实现基
于异步预取调度的数据预热机制。 首先， 利用训练框架随机种子固定所带来的访问序列
可预知性，在每轮训练启动前预计算出完整的文件访问顺序，并将之注入预取引擎；其
次， 预取引擎根据多因子优先级策略生成预取任务， 在带宽配额约束下将数据异步搬运
至本地缓存；最后，训练执行阶段预取引擎切换为后台运行模式，以预取窗口始终领先
于训练消费进度的方式持续搬运， 将原本串行加载转化为计算-加载流水线并行形式， 缓
解数据等待引起的算力浪费。

（3） 针对检查点同步写入阻塞训练迭代的问题， 设计并实现基于状态感知分配的检
查点分级写入机制。首先，构建覆盖边缘节点存储容量、实时带宽和拓扑距离等维度的
动态资源画像；其次，依据检查点分片的不同关键程度，划分四个优先等级，定制对应
匹配差异化副本策略；最后，通过异步本地写入将检查点快速落盘、训练随即恢复，远
端副本由后台线程自动完成； 故障恢复时依据优先等级就近取副本， 以最低传输代价恢
复训练状态。实验结果表明，该机制将写入阻塞时间压缩至与本地保存相当的水平，与
同步网络传输相比大幅缩短；分级恢复在各 RTT配置下均可稳定缩短故障恢复时间。
综上，本文面向云边端跨域分布式训练中目录遍历开销大、样本加载速度慢、检查
点写入阻塞严重等 IO性能瓶颈， 分别设计并实现了元数据批量预取与多级缓存机制、 基
于异步预取调度的数据预热机制以及基于状态感知分配的检查点分级写入机制， 有效优
化了跨域数据访问通路，提升了云边端跨域分布式训练效率。
关键词： 跨域分布式训练， 云边端协同， 分布式存储系统， 跨域数据访问， 广域网络

Abstract
As industrial digital transformation deepens, a three-tier heterogeneous computing 
topology comprising cloud data centers, edge clusters, and terminal devices has gradually taken 
shape. Cloud-edge-device collaborative cross-domain computing unifies compute and storage 
resources scattered across different network domains under coordinated scheduling, opening a 
new resource integration path for cross-domain distributed training. Accordingly, our research 
group developed CEDFS (Cloud-Edge-Device File System), a cloud-edge-device cross-domain 
virtual storage system that establishes a basic data access path among the three tiers.
However, wide-area links in cloud-edge-device environments commonly suffer from 
limited bandwidth and significant propagation delay, posing severe challenges to the I/O 
efficiency throughout the training workflow. Three prominent bottlenecks exist: first, metadata 
requests must frequently traverse cross-domain links, causing directory traversal and file 
attribute query overhead to multiply, which significantly delays training cold start; second, the 
rate of loading training samples from remote nodes cannot keep pace with GPU consumption, 
leaving compute units idle while waiting for data; third, synchronous checkpoint writes of large 
state files block training iterations for tens of seconds or even minutes, and the heterogeneous 
bandwidth and storage conditions across edge nodes make fast recovery after failures equally 
difficult to guarantee.
Therefore, targeting the I/O performance bottlenecks in cloud-edge-device cross-domain 
distributed training, this thesis conducts storage acceleration research along three paths: 
metadata batch prefetching, training data asynchronous prewarming, and tiered checkpoint 
writing and recovery, aiming to optimize cross-domain data access and improve training 
efficiency. The specific contributions and main results are as follows:
(1) To address the excessive overhead of cross-domain metadata access, a metadata 
batch prefetching and multi-level caching mechanism for training cold start is designed and 
implemented. During dataset initialization, full metadata is retrieved in a single round by 
combining asynchronous recursive directory tree scanning with request aggregation. A three-
level cache comprising memory, disk, and shared memory enables the prefetched metadata to

be directly reused in subsequent accesses, eliminating redundant network requests. Experiments 
show that the mechanism significantly reduces metadata access overhead across various file 
scales and latency conditions, achieving notable improvements in both cold start speed and 
cache hit efficiency in subsequent epochs.
(2) To address the problem of cross-domain sample loading slowing down training and 
causing GPU compute idleness, a data prewarming mechanism based on asynchronous prefetch 
scheduling is designed and implemented. Leveraging the access sequence predictability enabled 
by fixed random seeds in the training framework, the complete file access order is precomputed 
before each epoch and injected into the prefetch engine. The engine generates prefetch tasks 
according to a multi-factor priority policy and asynchronously transfers data to a local cache 
under bandwidth quota constraints. During training execution, the prefetch engine switches to 
background mode and continuously operates with a prefetch window that stays ahead of training 
consumption, transforming the originally serial loading into a computation-loading pipeline to 
alleviate compute waste caused by data stalls.
(3) To address training iteration blocking caused by synchronous checkpoint writing, a 
state-aware tiered checkpoint writing mechanism is designed and implemented. A dynamic 
resource profile covering edge node storage capacity, real-time bandwidth, and topological 
distance is constructed, and checkpoint shards are classified into four priority tiers based on 
their criticality for training recovery, each matched with a differentiated replication policy. An 
asynchronous local-first writing strategy persists checkpoints to NVMe at local disk speed, 
allowing training to resume immediately while remote replicas are completed by background 
threads; upon failure, replicas are retrieved from the nearest node according to their priority 
tier, minimizing transfer cost during recovery. Experiments show that the scheme reduces write 
blocking time to a level comparable to local saving, substantially shorter than synchronous 
network transmission. Tiered recovery consistently reduces fault recovery time across all RTT 
configurations.
In summary, targeting the I/O performance bottlenecks in cloud-edge-device cross-
domain distributed training, including high directory traversal overhead, slow sample loading, 
and severe checkpoint writing blockage, this thesis designs and implements metadata batch 
prefetching with multi-level caching, data prewarming based on asynchronous prefetch

1.2  研究背景与意义
在国家战略的推动下， 制造、 电网、 交通等关键产业正加速数智化转变[1]。 本文所研
究的“云边端环境”指由云端数据中心、边缘计算集群和终端设备构成的三层异构计算拓
扑。云边端协同跨域计算作为新兴模式，通过整合云边端环境中的异构资源，统筹不同
网络域的节点， 协同完成计算任务， 有效降低数据处理延迟和成本， 保护数据隐私安全，
这一模式为实现高效、高质的数字化转型注入了强大动力。如图 1 所示，云边端智能计
算生态涵盖智能应用、计算框架、存储资源、异构通信网络以及异构智算硬件等多个层
面，为分布式训练提供了丰富的基础设施支撑。
